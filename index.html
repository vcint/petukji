About Us

Petuk Ji Pvt. Ltd. is a UP Govt. funded startup that was chosen and awarded among top 100 startups in india by Google & Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, India ( MeitY). Company is based at Meerut, UP, India. This is a tech oriented company managed by Leaders with 15 Years + of great experience in various corporate domains and started its journey with its own B2B2C product cum service, named “Petuk Ji Food Ordering and Delivery Marketplace”.

Our Vision

To Connect Food Hawkers and food joints globally with rural & urban food lovers in their regional language to elevate their lifestyle with incomparable product and service mix.

Our Mission

To connect Food Hawkers, Food Joints and Food Lovers with petuk ji food delivery marketplace in such a way that food hawkers and food joints can enhance their revenue even after delivering quality food to food lovers in reasonable price with better support
